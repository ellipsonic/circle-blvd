About

Does your team know what it's doing this week? Where is your task list? On Basecamp? In shared spreadsheets? Email? In your paper notebook?

We've been there. We've tried that. That's why we made Circle Blvd.

Let's be frank: Circle Blvd is just a list. It's simple. It's not that smart. It doesn't do everything. And that's why it's good for your team.

We made this site to give your team just enough structure and glue, to stay on the same page between meetings, to keep you focused while you make something great.

Circle Blvd was created for teams who aren't necessarily sharing a day job. It's for on-the-side event organizers, or people starting businesses, or part-time community volunteers.

Circle Blvd is a path, to get your team where you want to go.

Product team

Circle Blvd is a product of Secret Project, a software development company based in Corvallis, Oregon.

Secret Project is led by Phil Manijak. Phil is a community organizer, co-founding the Corvallis Swing Dance Society, and a 10-year veteran of the software industry, with previous design and programming work used by millions of people across the globe.

Business plans

Our goal is to keep Circle Blvd free indefinitely, supported by donations and sponsors.

Our intention is to create a business model similar to Wordpress, in the way that it is free to download and modify, or you can rent out space on our site.

The source code for Circle Blvd available on GitHub, under a BSD license. It can be downloaded and installed on most computers running Mac OS X, Linux, or Windows.
